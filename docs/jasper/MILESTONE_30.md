# Jasper Milestone 30

## Primary Brain Region

Regulation & Growth.

## Objective

Make Jasper a self-sustaining personal intelligence system that compounds operator capability over years.

## Scope

This milestone is the long-range convergence point for reliability, memory, judgment, and autonomous execution.

Delivered here:

- always-on continuity with durable operator state and long-horizon adaptation
- self-review loops that keep Jasper aligned with the operator's real priorities
- resilience for hardware changes, service migration, and major runtime upgrades
- succession-safe exports and recovery of the operator's intelligence layer
- explicit measurement of whether Jasper is still delivering the `2X` promise

## Success Condition

Jasper feels less like an app and more like a persistent intelligence layer the operator can trust to remain useful, legible, and recoverable over the long run.

## Upstream Safety

Milestone 30 is a product convergence milestone. It should still prefer Jasper-owned behavior wherever possible, but it may justify deeper seams when those seams materially improve long-term continuity and trust.

## Verification

```bash
jasper continuity status
jasper system review
jasper export intelligence-layer
```

Expected outcome:

- Jasper remains recoverable and understandable after years of use
- the operator can measure whether Jasper is still amplifying real life
- continuity does not depend on one machine, one repo checkout, or one perfect session
